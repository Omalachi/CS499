# ePortfolio Development Overview – CS 499 Capstone

This repository outlines my work for developing a professional ePortfolio as part of the CS 499 Computer Science Capstone course. The purpose of this portfolio is to showcase my skills and growth as a computer science student, with a focus on real-world coding projects and enhancements that reflect my understanding of software development.

Creating this portfolio gives me a way to present my work to potential employers. It highlights my strengths in core areas of computer science, including software design and engineering, algorithms and data structures, and databases. Each artifact included was carefully selected to demonstrate my abilities in one or more of these areas.

For this project, I chose to enhance and review a Unity-based craps game that I originally developed in an earlier course. This artifact allowed me to demonstrate my skills across multiple categories, especially in software design and structure. As part of the capstone process, I improved the organization, modularity, and readability of the code, while also addressing limitations and preparing it for future development.

All enhancements go beyond the original course requirements and reflect my personal efforts to create a higher quality, more professional project. My portfolio reflects that I am committed, well-prepared, and ready to contribute as a developer in the field of computer science.
